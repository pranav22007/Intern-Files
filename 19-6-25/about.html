About Us......

The CSMSS College of Polytechnic is a technical-education institute in the city of Aurangabad, Maharashtra, India. It was established in 2009 with 5 diploma programs and a sanctioned intake of 390 students. The institute is affiliated with the MSBTE and managed by the CSMSS. The college takes its name from a ruler of the Maratha Empire, Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj. It has been accredited by the DTE The Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Shikshan Sanstha (CSMSS) ("Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Institute of Education") was established in 1986 and was named after a renowned ruler and social worker of the Maratha Empire - Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj.
